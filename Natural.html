Natural Causes of Climate Change

Major Natural Causes of Climate Change

  • Volcanic Eruptions

    Volcanic eruptions release large amounts of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, contributing to global warming. They also emit sulfur compounds that can reflect sunlight and cause temporary cooling.

  • Meteors

    When meteors enter the Earth's atmosphere, they can cause significant heating and contribute to climate change through the release of energy and particles. When They Strike The ground they can cause lots of dust to fly in the air which can block out the sun For Example: When The meteor that killed the dinosaurs struck the Earth it caused a massive climate change by blocking out the sun and causing starvation

  • Solar Variations

    Changes in the Sun's energy output can affect Earth's climate. While these variations are relatively small, they can still contribute to long-term climate changes. For example, a decrease in solar activity coupled with increased volcanic activity helped trigger the Little Ice Age.
